About Dot

Grace and Kehinde

DOT is a young, London based company co-owned by Grace Hooper and Kehinde Akinnawo who met whilst working in the Ralph Lauren press office.

Kehinde graduated from The London College of Fashion with a degree in fashion illustration. She has worked in PR for publications and fashion houses including Wonderland, Twelv, Moschino, Jimmy Choo and Burberry.

With a degree in Art History from Leeds University, Grace worked at Conde Nast in editorial for magazines including Tatler, World of Interiors and international titles. She also worked for a small PR agency, gaining clients day to day press coverage and organising events.
