About Dot

Dot comprises a group of energetic, hardworking and knowledgeable professionals with deep connections to the fashion and media arena, and stellar proclivities in public relations, branding/marketing and production. Dot works with emerging and established brands in the fashion, retail and the lifestyle sector to meet their specific needs. We ideate and execute sharp, imaginative and effective solutions to help brands deliver on their intended goals.

The creation of Dot

Dot was founded by Grace Hooper and Kehinde Akinnawo who met whilst working in the Ralph Lauren press office.

Kehinde graduated from The London College of Fashion with a degree in fashion illustration. She has worked in PR for publications and fashion houses including Wonderland, Twelv, Moschino, Jimmy Choo and Burberry.

Grace holds a degree in Art History from Leeds University, Grace worked at Conde Nast in editorial for magazines including Tatler, World of Interiors and other international titles, going on to work at Ralph Lauren in the press department.

Dot has now grown into a full services PR firm specialising in two broad areas, Brand Communication & Engagement and Fashion Consulting & Development. We wanted to work with emerging and established brands to make good use of their promotional budgets. With our broad network, deep know-how, and immense experience, we help brands, designers and creators in fashion and lifestyle enter and/or solidify a presence in a crowded market.
