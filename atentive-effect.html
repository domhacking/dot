Atentive Effect

You don’t have to be a Atentive PR client for us to organise an event for you. We can provide a total event package or we can work alongside your team, looking after whatever areas you need our help with.

Events can be tricky for small brands to execute well, so we work closely with clients to understand what you want to gain from the event. Confirming guests’ attendance at events can be difficult due to the number of invitations they receive, so we create a strong lead up campaign across social media channels and make sure that the guests are going to be actively interested in your product.

From consultancy and creating guest lists to conception and complete event organisation, from catwalk shows to press dinners and everything in between, Atentive handles all aspects of events.
