About us

Dot is unique – because we are both a public relations and an events company, and clients can either tap into one or both of our services. We are flexible working with you – we will take on whole projects or we will back up your PR and events teams.

Dot is a young London based company co-owned by Grace Hooper and Kehinde Akinnawo. We are bright, energetic, hardworking people with a background in magazines and fashion. With our personal connections we help brands, designers and creators in fashion and lifestyle to launch or maintain a presence in a crowded market.

Unlike traditional PR and events companies we don't have binding formal arrangements with our clients – instead we come up flexible contracts to suit budgets, allowing for easy, organic growth while helping to gain the coverage they want and need.

From simply boosting notoriety to introducing a new product to the market, we can build a bespoke PR and events plan for you.
