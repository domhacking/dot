Joy

Nationwide fashion retailer Joy came to Dot with outline plans for their SS16 press day. Working with their in-house team we put forward ideas to make the event more interesting and run more smoothly.

We came up with the theme of a secret garden to reflect the patterns and motifs in their summer collection, and decided on 15 Bateman Street in Soho as a venue that guests would find convenient and appealing. We then organised the content, decoration, guest list and goodie bag.

Creative ideas evolved and our initial plan to include a tree prop in the centre of the space became over ambitious in the short time frame. We went back to the drawing board and decided on a plan B, to make a wall hanging of paper flowers.

The goodie bag was a key part of our brief - we contacted companies for products which we felt aligned with Joy’s profile including Man Gun Bear, Joe and Seph’s Popcorn, The London Tea Company, Percol Coffee, Big Hug Brewing Beer. Guests also enjoyed a manicure by Secret Spa.

More than 60 guests from all parts of the media came to the event.
