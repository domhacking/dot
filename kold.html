Kold

Kold

Kold Cocktails came to Dot with an open book. They hadn't officially launched since starting their business and wanted to introduce their unique frozen cocktails to the press with a pre-launch event.

We homed in on London Fashion Week as a prime moment to reach international and UK press who were all gathered in the capital. Ideas ranged from an ice themed party at London’s Ice Bar to a cocktail tea party - finally we chose an evening ‘pamper parlour’ at the ME Hotel on Aldwych with participating beauty companies Little Ondine, Duck & Dry, Secret Spa, Shavata and Bohemian Jewellery.

The guest list included Brides, The Telegraph, Evening Standard, OK! and many more as well as fashion bloggers including La Petite Anglaise and celebrities Louise Thompson from Made in Chelsea.

Everyone left with a full goody bag including Essie nail varnishes, Deciem beauty products, Ombar chocolate, Proper Corn popcorn, London Tea Company tea, Vita Liberata tanning products, Blend Collective hand creams, Wheelie taxi vouchers - and of course Kold Cocktails. We gathered all of these full sized product samples at no cost to our client, and simultaneously delighted our guests.

The press showed great interest in Kold Cocktails as a result of the Dot event, and productive promotion gained 1000 new followers across social media channels.
