PR

Atentive has the contacts to reach magazines, newspapers, celebrities, bloggers and vloggers who will consider featuring your business across all media and social channels. We can put your brand in front of the right people. PR informs your consumer and create a buzz around your product which in turn builds your brand identity, leading to sales.

Atentive offers 3 flexible possibilities, all with monthly reports and detailed reviews:

  • Project
  • Monthly
  • Long term

Project

If you have a product launch, a new range, an event or need PR for a one off project, we can work with you in the lead up to it, helping you gain the desired media coverage.

Monthly

If you need monthly PR, you can sign with us for a month at a time, meaning you can have the option of a break at the end of each month. We can work with you on a project basis to start and then switch to a monthly option.

Long term

Our six month contract gives us the time to build a presence for your brand with the media both during seasonal campaigns and in between.
